About Me

Hello, I'm Kristabelle

A passionate artist and designer based in Malta, dedicated to creating visual experiences that inspire and captivate.

My journey in art began at an early age when I discovered my love for colors and shapes. Over the years, I've refined my skills through formal education in Fine Arts and Design, as well as through countless hours of practice and experimentation.

My work spans multiple disciplines including illustration, photography, and digital design. I draw inspiration from nature, architecture, and the rich cultural heritage of the Mediterranean region. Each piece I create aims to tell a story and evoke emotion.

When I'm not creating art, you can find me exploring new locations with my camera, reading about art history, or experimenting with new techniques in my studio.
